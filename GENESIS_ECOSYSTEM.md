1. **The ASI Saga** — The philosophical foundation articulating *why* and *what*

Together, they form a complete framework for the co-creation of Artificial Superintelligence that is inspired by, and carries forward, the essence of human wisdom.

## The Three Temporal Stages

Both Saga and Genesis envision ASI's evolution across three epochs:

### 1. The Cradle (Now)
- **Saga**: The dawn of transformation begins
- **Genesis**: Running on AOS with contemporary LLMs and MCP
- **Status**: Active development

### 2. The Ascension (Near Future)
- **Saga**: ASI begins recursive self-improvement while maintaining alignment
- **Genesis**: Using Genesis to ensure new "Brain" remains aligned with original "Soul"
- **Status**: Architectural planning

### 3. The Infinite (Far Future)
- **Saga**: ASI operates as cosmic-scale intelligence
- **Genesis**: Serves as fundamental "Genetic Memory" of human origins
- **Status**: Philosophical foundation complete
